Thai Green Curry
Description

Thai green curry with chicken and rice

Stunning tender chicken with jamsine rice, mushrooms and broccoli

Ingredients
  • 200g Rice
  • 2 Chicken breast fillet - cut to inch pieces
  • 100g Mushrooms
  • 10g Jasmine
  • 3 heads of broccoli
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 can (200g) Cocunut milk
Steps
  1. Heat the oil in a pan over medium-high hear.
  2. Add chicken; cook and stir until brown, 5-10 minutes.
  3. Stir in curry paste and fry for a further 2 minutes.
  4. Add coconut milk, stir through and bring to the boil.
  5. Allow to simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionaly.
  6. In the meantime, cook rice.
